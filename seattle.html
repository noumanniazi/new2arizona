So why are people leaving Seattle, Washington

People are leaving Seattle, Washington, due to rising living costs, housing affordability issues, and concerns over public safety. The city's thriving tech industry has significantly increased property prices and rental costs, making it challenging for many residents to find affordable housing. Additionally, Seattle has faced increasing issues related to homelessness and crime, which have impacted the overall quality of life. Some residents are also frustrated with the city's traffic congestion and the perceived lack of effective policy solutions to these problems. As a result, many are opting to relocate to areas with a lower cost of living, better housing options, and a more stable and secure environment.
